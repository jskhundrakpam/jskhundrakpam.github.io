There are dates you look forward to, and then there are dates that redefine your entire existence. For me, that date is today.

![Francis at RIMS](/assets/img/son.jpg)

After years of waiting, hoping, and dreaming, I finally held my son in my arms. The world stopped for a moment. Everything I thought I knew about love was rewritten in a single heartbeat.

I remember his tiny fingers, the warmth of his breath, and the overwhelming sense of responsibility mixed with pure joy. It wasn't just a meeting – it was the beginning of a new version of myself. A father.

This page is a small monument to that day. To every parent who has felt the same, and to every child who makes life worth living.

— *Johnson, forever grateful.*
